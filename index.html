What it does

10 tools. One strategic brain that already knows your newsletter.

audit_substack
Run this once. Donna reads your newsletter and builds your Creator Portrait — your voice, your positioning gaps, your content patterns, your audience profile. Everything else runs from this. No audit, no context. This is where you start.
suggest_topics
3–5 topic ideas drawn from your strategy, your audience's real pains, and your current flywheel phase. Not generic brainstorm fodder — ideas with a reason behind each one.
write_note
150–280 word Substack Notes in your voice. Punchy. Opinionated. Ready to post. Donna writes it as you — not as a content manager approximating you.
write_essay
Full 800–1,200 word essays. Structure planned before a word is written. SEO mode included. Built around your positioning, not a blank brief.
write_hook
3 opening lines for any topic. Different angles. Different energy. You pick the one that fits — or steal parts from all three.
write_comment_reply
Replies that earn attention — they add a perspective, sharpen a point, or name something the original post left unsaid. Sounds like you. Not a community manager template.
build_content_plan
A 30-day plan of essays, notes, and themes built around your actual posting cadence — not what a content calendar template assumes you should be doing.
check_strategy
The single highest-leverage correction available to you right now. No padding.
analyze_signal
Paste your engagement data. Get a plain-English read on what it means, what's working, and exactly what to do next.
log_decision
When you make a strategic pivot, log it here. Donna formats it and tells you how it changes your content going forward — so the context stays current.
